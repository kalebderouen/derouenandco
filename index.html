Our Story

Two people.
One shared vision.

We met at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette — two students from the same part of the state who found each other and never looked back. In 2021, Kaleb moved to New Orleans for medical school at LSU Health, and Malorie followed in 2022. When Kaleb matched into neurosurgery residency here at LSU, we knew this city was home for good.

Kaleb is a neurosurgery resident and Malorie is a registered nurse. Our professional lives demand precision, accountability, and genuine care for the people we serve. Those values don't stay at the hospital — they shape every business relationship and partnership we enter into here in New Orleans.

"New Orleans didn't just become our home. It became our reason to build something."
